-------------------------------------------------------------
WebForm application with AccessDB
-------------------------------------------------------------

To connect the database with your Visual Studio first open your Visual studio,
then choose my version of a WebForm app and double click on the web.config file.
There you can see the ConnectionString and change it with your local location of
the Database file.
In the Retrieve form the Edit and Delete buttons are working perfectly,
but the Insert button makes some problems.
The Login form isn's also the best.
